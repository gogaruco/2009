Finally!

San Francisco is the birthplace of the web revolution. Ruby powers many new technologies, from web sites to telephony and desktop applications. Now, at long last, San Francisco is host to the Bay Area's own Ruby Conference.

Join 200 of your fellow developers for two days of in-depth technical explorations of the most interesting parts of the Ruby landscape, presented by some of the top talents in the Ruby development community.

Register Online

Registration is now open! The cost for the 2-day conference is $199 and includes lunch both days, some cool swag, and entry to any evening social events that may turn up. We have space for 200 seats, so register now to reserve yours.

Ruby Application Frameworks Panel

We're really proud of the speakers we've been able to line up for you, and they'll be giving some great talks. But the highlight of the technical program will be the Ruby Application Frameworks discussion panel. Six of the major Ruby application frameworks will be represented by their authors or contributors: Rails, Merb, Sinatra, Adhearsion, RAD, and of course Shoes.
